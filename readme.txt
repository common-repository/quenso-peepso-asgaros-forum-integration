Integrate Asgaros Forum in your PeepSo community.

== Description ==
This plugin connects the features of the best forum plugin for WordPress with [PeepSo](https://peepso.com/ "PeepSo - Social Network Plugin for WordPress") for free. It brings some notification and activity extras and ease up the way to to use [Asgaros Forum](https://asgaros.de/ "Asgaros Forum- The best forum plugin for WordPress") in your community.

= Notifications And Activity Stream =
Sends notifications for new post and topics to your members notification system. Gives you also options to show forum activities of your members in the activity stream and in user profiles.

= Fully Integrated Profiles (Premium Feature) =
Fully integrates forum stats and data into PeepSo profiles. See members activities in the stream, lists their posts and topics in a new tab and gives your members the ability to change subscription settings in their profiles.

= Forum Appearance (Premium Feature) =
Make Asgaros Forum and PeepSo one big community. Add the PeepSo Toolbar to the header of your forum page and show forum related links inside the navigation of your PeepSo community.

= Some Features of the integration: =
* Create activities and notifications for forum activities in PeepSo
* Redirect forum profiles to PeepSo profiles
* Member can change their forum notification settings inside their PeepSo profiles

= Premium Features =
* Show forum stats of members inside their PeepSo profile
* Notifications for mentions and reactions
* Fully integrates subscription settings in your members profiles
* Show the PeepSo toolbar in forum pages to make your forum and PeepSo one big community
* Forum related links can be shown in the navigation of PeepSo

== Installation ==
* Make sure that `PeepSo` and `Asgaros Forum` are installed and activated
* Download `PeepSo - Asgaros Forum Integration by Quenso` from plugin directory in WordPress
* Activate the plugin via `Plugins` screen in WordPress backend
* Change settings in `WordPress Backend -> PeepSo -> Configuration -> Asgaros Forum Integration` to your desired ones
* Thats all - have fun!
